Case Study

The Performer's Dilemma: Finding the Perfect Fit

The Challenge

Alex, a professional fetish performer, had spent thousands on off-the-rack latex catsuits that never quite fit right. Standard sizes were too tight in the shoulders, too loose in the waist, and restricted movement during performances. After trying 5 different brands, they were ready to give up on latex entirely.

Our Solution

We took 28 precise measurements and created a custom pattern specifically for Alex's body. Using 0.4mm latex in their signature purple with a high-visibility reflective stripe for stage presence, we crafted a catsuit with reinforced seams at stress points and a unique back zipper design for quick changes.
